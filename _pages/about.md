I am a PhD student in Economics & Business at the University of Michigan, jointly in the Department of Economics and the Ross School of Business. My research interests are in public, labor, and family economics.

I am currently working on a project titled "Investigating the Genetic and Environmental Influences on Income using US Census and IRS Data," joint with Jonathan Beauchamp, Jennifer Bernard, and Amanda Kowalski. I am a 2026 dissertation mentorship participant at the US Census Bureau Center for Economic Studies under Jennifer Bernard.

You can find my CV [here](/cv/) and contact me at [rcfraser@umich.edu](mailto:rcfraser@umich.edu).
